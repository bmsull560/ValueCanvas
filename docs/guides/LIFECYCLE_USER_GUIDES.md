# Lifecycle Workflow User Guides (Opportunity → Expansion)

These guides help business users run each lifecycle phase end-to-end using the ValueCanvas experiences and Agent Fabric outputs.

## Opportunity Stage
- **Goal**: Capture customer context, pains, and initial KPIs.
- **Inputs**: Discovery notes/transcripts, target persona, industry segment.
- **How to run**:
  1. Open the Command Bar (`⌘K`/`Ctrl+K`) and select **"Run Opportunity Agent"**.
  2. Paste discovery notes and specify industry + persona.
  3. Review generated discovery questions and KPI hypotheses in the canvas sidebar.
- **Outputs to expect**: Pain → KPI mappings, persona fit score, recommended next discovery actions.

## Target Stage
- **Goal**: Convert opportunity signals into quantifiable targets and ROI narratives.
- **Inputs**: Opportunity output, baseline KPIs, benchmark references.
- **How to run**:
  1. From the Opportunity canvas, click **"Promote to Target"**.
  2. Confirm baselines and choose a ROI model template.
  3. Let the Target Agent generate target KPIs, value commits, and ROI models.
- **Outputs to expect**: Target KPIs with confidence, benchmark comparisons, initial ROI/payback summary.

## Realization Stage
- **Goal**: Track live performance against targets and generate reports.
- **Inputs**: Telemetry events, KPI baselines/targets, time window.
- **How to run**:
  1. Upload telemetry or connect the data source for the account.
  2. Open **Realization Agent** and select the reporting period.
  3. Generate a Realization Report and review variance explanations.
- **Outputs to expect**: Variance analysis, realization snapshots, executive summary for QBRs.

## Expansion Stage
- **Goal**: Identify upsell/cross-sell opportunities and quantify expansion value.
- **Inputs**: Latest Realization Report, open gaps, account goals.
- **How to run**:
  1. From a Realization Report, click **"Evaluate Expansion"**.
  2. The Expansion Agent scores opportunity size and proposes packages.
  3. Export the Expansion Summary for sales follow-up.
- **Outputs to expect**: Opportunity score, recommended improvements, projected expansion ROI.

## Integrity Checks (Always-On)
- **Goal**: Ensure manifesto compliance, provenance, and explainability.
- **How to use**: From any stage, open the **Integrity Panel** to view rule evaluations, remediation guidance, and evidence links. Resolve blockers before promoting artifacts to the next stage.

## Tips for Business Users
- Keep inputs concise and source-linked to improve explainability scores.
- Use bookmarks in the documentation portal for templates that match your industry.
- Re-run agents after major assumption changes to refresh ROI and compliance metadata.
